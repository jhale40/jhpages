Battle of The Big Three

Introduction

One of my favorite hobbies and passions is for cars. The unique blend of performance and style are key to producing a successful and desirable product in a competitive market. America the good old U.S.A has done a great job of this. The Big Three American automotive manufacturers Chevrolet, Ford and Dodge have created an iconic 60 year rivalry for who will be King of the streets. I personally love all three of them but I do have a little bias toward Chevrolet. When I was younger I was upset that I missed the battle of muscle cars in the 1960s and 1970s. But then a new era of super muscle cars started to develop in the twenty first century that continues to push the standard of performance to a new level each year.
